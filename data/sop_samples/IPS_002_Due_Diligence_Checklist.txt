DUE DILIGENCE CHECKLIST FOR FUND INVESTMENTS
Document ID: IPS-002
Effective Date: 2024-03-01
Last Review: 2025-06-01
Owner: Director of Research

1. INVESTMENT DUE DILIGENCE (IDD)

1.1 Strategy Assessment:
- [ ] Clearly defined and documented investment strategy
- [ ] Strategy consistent with our portfolio needs and allocation targets
- [ ] Market opportunity analysis for the strategy
- [ ] Competitive landscape assessment -- how does the fund differentiate?
- [ ] Capacity analysis -- can the fund accept our allocation without diluting returns?

1.2 Track Record Review:
- [ ] Minimum 3-year audited track record
- [ ] Gross and net returns attribution
- [ ] Performance in up and down markets (asymmetry analysis)
- [ ] Comparison to relevant benchmark and peer group
- [ ] Sharpe ratio, Sortino ratio, max drawdown analysis
- [ ] Return persistence -- are returns repeatable or driven by one-time events?

1.3 Portfolio Construction:
- [ ] Position sizing methodology documented
- [ ] Concentration risk assessment (top 10 positions)
- [ ] Sector and geographic diversification review
- [ ] Liquidity profile of underlying positions
- [ ] Use of leverage -- terms, limits, and historical usage
- [ ] Hedging approach and effectiveness

1.4 Terms Review:
- [ ] Management fee structure (typical: 1-2%)
- [ ] Performance fee / carry (typical: 20%, with hurdle)
- [ ] Hurdle rate and catch-up provisions
- [ ] Liquidity terms (lock-up, redemption notice, gates)
- [ ] Side pocket provisions
- [ ] Most Favored Nation (MFN) clause availability
- [ ] Key-person clause and trigger events

2. OPERATIONAL DUE DILIGENCE (ODD)

2.1 Firm Structure:
- [ ] Legal entity structure and jurisdictions
- [ ] Ownership and succession plan
- [ ] Regulatory registration (SEC, FCA, etc.)
- [ ] Insurance coverage (E&O, D&O, fidelity bond)
- [ ] Business continuity and disaster recovery plan

2.2 Team Assessment:
- [ ] Key investment professionals -- tenure, experience, track record
- [ ] Key-person risk evaluation
- [ ] Compensation structure and alignment
- [ ] Staff turnover history
- [ ] Succession planning documentation

2.3 Operations:
- [ ] Independent administrator and auditor
- [ ] Valuation policy and procedures
- [ ] Trade execution and best execution policy
- [ ] Compliance program and CCO background
- [ ] Technology infrastructure assessment
- [ ] Cybersecurity policies and testing

2.4 Reporting:
- [ ] Monthly NAV reporting with transparency requirements
- [ ] Quarterly investor letter with attribution
- [ ] Annual audited financial statements
- [ ] Tax reporting (K-1 timing for US funds)

3. ESG ASSESSMENT

- [ ] ESG policy documented and integrated into investment process
- [ ] UN PRI signatory or equivalent commitment
- [ ] ESG reporting capabilities
- [ ] Controversial sectors screening policy
- [ ] Climate risk assessment for portfolio

4. REFERENCE CHECKS

- [ ] Minimum 3 investor references (existing LPs)
- [ ] Service provider references (administrator, auditor, prime broker)
- [ ] Background checks on key principals
- [ ] Litigation and regulatory history review

5. APPROVAL PROCESS

5.1 Research Team: Completes IDD and ODD checklists, prepares investment memo
5.2 CIO Review: Reviews memo and checklist, provides recommendation
5.3 Investment Committee: Approves or rejects based on presentation
5.4 Legal Review: Reviews subscription documents and side letter
5.5 Compliance: Final sign-off on regulatory and policy compliance
